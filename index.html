How it works

Add your services

Add your services by adding a title and a description. Then you fix a price, set duration for the session. Your clients will be able to discover services on your page.

Share your profile

Your profile can be accessible at username.10fold.in. Your clients can explore your services on your profile page and book appointments with you.

Automatic session reminders

10 minutes before the session begins, you and your clients will receive SMS with a meeting link. All the meetings are fully encrypted. We use Google Meet and Zoom to run these meetings for you.

Take full control over your time

You always have full control on when your clients can take appointments with you. By eliminating unnecessary discussions between you and your clients, 10fold saves time for you and help you be more productive in your life.

Use Cases

Yoga and Wellness

Close more bookings, and free up your time to do what you do best. Manage your time, sell your sessions and appointments. Check-in your clients and keep a track of their attendance.

Dance Studio

10fold is a simple, flexible app built for dance instructors. You can build your dance schedule by specifying your available slots. Your students can view real-time availability, confirm bookings and payments.

Tuitions

Take your classes online with 10fold. Teach students in group as well as 1-on-1, or solve their doubt in separate doubt classes. Easily collect fee and track attendance. 10fold works with Zoom, Jio Meet and all other video tools.

Doctors and Therapists

10fold is designed to support all your booking requirements. Let your patient book appointments and pay you online. Sell your expertise and build your profile.

Retails

10fold can help you increase your turnover by enabling your customers to pre-book services online. They can check available slots, take appointments with your staff and pay online for the service.
