Last updated: September 1, 2020

10fold subscription plan

If you are not satisfied with 10fold within the first 7 days of your paid subscription, we will happily provide you with a full refund for your initial subscription payment. This is available only to new customers who have never taken a paid subscription before.

Bookings Payment

Successful bookings made through 10fold are refundable fully or partially based on the duration in which they are cancelled. For more information, please read terms on the booking page.
